# Examples — SaaS / Email Marketing

## Micro-Insights

```
Email list > Instagram following.
Your list is yours. The algorithm isn't.
Most lists die from silence, not unsubscribes.
Every unsent email is money left on the table.
Send the email. Stop rehearsing it.
Bad subject lines kill good emails.
Open rate is a vanity metric. Revenue isn't.
Data-driven emails outperform feelings-driven ones.
The welcome email sets the tone for everything.
AI doesn't replace your voice. It sharpens it.
```

## Relatable List

```
Email marketing in 2026:
- Open Mailchimp.
- Stare at blank subject line.
- Close Mailchimp.
- Tell yourself you'll do it Friday.
- It's been 3 months.

Meanwhile your competitors just hit send and booked 12 clients.

Tag the business owner who's been "almost ready to start" since January.
```

Small business email marketing journey:

- Watch 4 tutorials.

- Write the email.

- Blame the platform.

The platform was fine.
