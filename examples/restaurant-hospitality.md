# Examples — Restaurant & Hospitality

## Micro-Insights

```
One expired permit closes everything.
The fine is always bigger than the fee.
Inspectors don't care about your excuses.
Compliance isn't optional. It's survival.
Permits expire. Surprises don't.
Restaurant owners don't fail on food. They fail on margins.
A full house doesn't guarantee a profitable night.
Staff turnover kills restaurants faster than bad reviews.
You didn't open a restaurant to do paperwork. And yet.
Nobody becomes a chef to manage a spreadsheet.
Passion got you in. Systems keep you open.
The kitchen is the easy part.
```
